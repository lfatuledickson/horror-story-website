About Me

Hi, my author name is EndlessBern, and I write short horror stories as a hobby.
I have decided to put my writing skills to good use and make this website to bring
everyone some chills late at night.

If you like my works, please follow me on my social networks, and feel free to support me
by buying my merch. Purchasing a monthly subscription will grant you full access to all my
works. A 12-month subscription will additionally give you an opportunity once a year to
work with me in your own personal horror story.
